Privacy Policy  ·  Effective April 14, 2026

Thank you for using Cashout. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use the App, we may collect:

  • Phone number (used for account verification)
  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Email address (optional, for prize notifications)
  • Payment information for prize payouts (processed securely via Stripe)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Usage data (game scores, streaks, session length)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Firebase Analytics data (app performance and crash reports)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Operate and improve the App and daily trivia game
  • Process and deliver prize payouts to winners
  • Send game reminders and prize notifications
  • Display leaderboards and player rankings
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair play

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and analytics
  • Stripe — secure payment processing for prize payouts
  • Apple — App Store distribution

3.2 Sponsors

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable data (such as total player count and engagement metrics) with our sponsors for reporting purposes. We do not share your personal contact information with sponsors without your explicit consent.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Prize Payouts and Financial Data

If you win a prize, we will collect the information necessary to process your payout through Stripe, which may include:

  • Bank account or debit card information
  • Government-issued ID (for prizes over $600, as required by US tax law)
  • Social Security Number or Tax ID (for IRS Form 1099 reporting, if applicable)

All financial data is processed and stored securely by Stripe. Winners of $600 or more in a calendar year may receive an IRS Form 1099 and are responsible for reporting prize income on their tax returns.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us at privacy@cashoutplay.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Stripe's PCI-compliant payment processing
  • Access controls limiting who can view user data

8. Your Rights and Choices

You may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out — opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@cashoutplay.com.

9. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications for daily games, prize wins, and streak reminders. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the effective date and sending a notification through the App.
